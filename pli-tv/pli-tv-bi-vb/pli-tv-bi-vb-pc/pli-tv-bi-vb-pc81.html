Kumāribhūtavagga The sub-chapter on unmarried girls

81. Pārivāsikasikkhāpada 81. The training rule on what is expired

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī— At that time the nun Thullanandā assembled a group of senior monks, “sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti there bhikkhū sannipātetvā pahūtaṃ khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyaṃ passitvā— intending to give the full ordination to a trainee nun. But after seeing much staple and non-staple food, she dismissed the senior monks, saying, “na tāvāhaṃ, ayyā, sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti there bhikkhū uyyojetvā devadattaṃ kokālikaṃ kaṭamodakatissakaṃ khaṇḍadeviyā puttaṃ samuddadattaṃ sannipātetvā sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpesi. “Venerables, I won’t give the full ordination to the trainee nun just yet.” She then assembled Devadatta, Kokālika, Kaṭamodakatissaka, Khaṇḍadeviyā-putta, and Samuddadatta, and gave the full ordination to that trainee nun.

Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The nuns of few desires complained and criticized her, “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā pārivāsikachandadānena sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessatī”ti … pe … “How could Venerable Thullanandā, with expired consent, give the full ordination to a trainee nun?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī pārivāsikachandadānena sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpetī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that the nun Thullanandā did this?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized her, “… kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī pārivāsikachandadānena sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessati. How can the nun Thullanandā, with expired consent, give the full ordination to a trainee nun? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Yā pana bhikkhunī pārivāsikachandadānena sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyan”ti. ‘If, with expired consent, a nun gives the full ordination to a trainee nun, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Yā panāti A:
yā yādisā … pe … whoever …
bhikkhunīti Nun:
… pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti. … The nun who has been given the full ordination by both complete Orders through a legal procedure consisting of one motion and three announcements that is irreversible and fit to stand—this sort of nun is meant in this case.
Pārivāsikachandadānenāti With expired consent:
vuṭṭhitāya parisāya. with an assembly that has left.
Sikkhamānā nāma A trainee nun:
dve vassāni chasu dhammesu sikkhitasikkhā. one who has trained for two years in the six rules.
Vuṭṭhāpeyyāti Gives the full ordination to:
upasampādeyya. gives the full ordination to. “Vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti gaṇaṃ vā ācariniṃ vā pattaṃ vā cīvaraṃ vā pariyesati, sīmaṃ vā sammannati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. If, intending to give the full ordination, she searches for a group, a teacher, a bowl, or a robe, or she establishes a boundary, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Ñattiyā dukkaṭaṃ. After the motion, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Dvīhi kammavācāhi dukkaṭā. After each of the first two announcements, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Kammavācāpariyosāne upajjhāyāya āpatti pācittiyassa. When the last announcement is finished, the preceptor commits an offense entailing confession; Gaṇassa ca ācariniyā ca āpatti dukkaṭassa. the group and the teacher commit an offense of wrong conduct.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: avuṭṭhitāya parisāya vuṭṭhāpeti, if she give her the full ordination when the assembly has not left; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. The eleventh training rule is finished.
