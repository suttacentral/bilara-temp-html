Bhikkhunivibhaṅga The nuns’ rules and their analysis

Pācittiyakaṇḍa The chapter on offenses entailing confession

Kumāribhūtavagga The sub-chapter on unmarried girls

80. Ananuññātasikkhāpada 80. The training rule on lack of permission

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī mātāpitūhipi sāmikenapi ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpeti. At that time the nun Thullanandā gave the full ordination to a trainee nun who had not been given permission by her parents and her husband. Mātāpitaropi sāmikopi ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The parents and the husband complained and criticized her, “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā amhehi ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessatī”ti. “How could Venerable Thullanandā give the full ordination to a trainee nun without our permission?”

Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo mātāpitūnampi sāmikassapi ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. The nuns heard the complaints of the parents and the husband. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The nuns of few desires complained and criticized her, “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā mātāpitūhipi sāmikenapi ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessatī”ti … pe … “How could Venerable Thullanandā give the full ordination to a trainee nun who does not have permission from her parents and her husband?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī, mātāpitūhipi sāmikenapi ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpetī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that the nun Thullanandā did this?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized her, “… kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī mātāpitūhipi sāmikenapi ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpessati. How can the nun Thullanandā give the full ordination to a trainee nun who does not have permission from her parents and her husband? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Yā pana bhikkhunī mātāpitūhi vā sāmikena vā ananuññātaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyan”ti. ‘If a nun gives the full ordination to a trainee nun who has not been given permission by her mother and father or her husband, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Yā panāti A:
yā yādisā … pe … whoever …
bhikkhunīti Nun:
… pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti. … The nun who has been given the full ordination by both complete Orders through a legal procedure consisting of one motion and three announcements that is irreversible and fit to stand—this sort of nun is meant in this case.
Mātāpitaro nāma Mother and father:
janakā vuccanti. the biological parents is what is meant.
Sāmiko nāma Husband:
yena pariggahitā hoti. he who possesses her.
Ananuññātāti Who has not been given permission:
anāpucchā. who has not asked.
Sikkhamānā nāma A trainee nun:
dve vassāni chasu dhammesu sikkhitasikkhā. one who has trained for two years in the six rules.
Vuṭṭhāpeyyāti Gives the full ordination to:
upasampādeyya. gives the full ordination to. “Vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti gaṇaṃ vā ācariniṃ vā pattaṃ vā cīvaraṃ vā pariyesati, sīmaṃ vā sammannati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. If, intending to give the full ordination, she searches for a group, a teacher, a bowl, or a robe, or she establishes a boundary, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Ñattiyā dukkaṭaṃ. After the motion, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Dvīhi kammavācāhi dukkaṭā. After each of the first two announcements, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Kammavācāpariyosāne upajjhāyāya āpatti pācittiyassa. When the last announcement is finished, the preceptor commits an offense entailing confession; Gaṇassa ca ācariniyā ca āpatti dukkaṭassa. the group and the teacher commit an offense of wrong conduct.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: ajānantī vuṭṭhāpeti, if she gives her the full ordination without knowing; apaloketvā vuṭṭhāpeti, if she gives her the full ordination after asking them; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. The tenth training rule is finished.
