Pācittiyakaṇḍa The chapter on offenses entailing confession

Lasuṇavagga The sub-chapter on garlic

10. Naccagītasikkhāpada 10. The training rule on dancing and singing

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. At one time the Buddha was staying at Rājagaha in the Bamboo Grove, the squirrel sanctuary. Tena kho pana samayena rājagahe giraggasamajjo hoti. At that time in Rājagaha there was a hilltop festival, Chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo giraggasamajjaṃ dassanāya agamaṃsu. and the nuns from the group of six went to see it. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— People complained and criticized them, “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo naccampi gītampi vāditampi dassanāya gacchissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti. “How can nuns go to see dancing, singing, and music? They are just like lay people who indulge in sensual pleasures.”

Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. The nuns heard the complaints of those people, Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— and the nuns of few desires complained and criticized them, “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo naccampi gītampi vāditampi dassanāya gacchissantī”ti … pe … “How can the nuns from the group of six go to see dancing, singing, and music?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo naccampi gītampi vāditampi dassanāya gacchantī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that those nuns did that?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized them, kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo naccampi gītampi vāditampi dassanāya gacchissanti. “How can the nuns from the group of six go to see dancing, singing, and music? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Yā pana bhikkhunī naccaṃ vā gītaṃ vā vāditaṃ vā dassanāya gaccheyya, pācittiyan”ti. ‘If a nun goes to see dancing or singing or music, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Yā panāti A:
yā yādisā … pe … whoever …
bhikkhunīti Nun:
… pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti. … The nun who has been given the full ordination by both complete Orders through a legal procedure consisting of one motion and three announcements that is irreversible and fit to stand—this sort of nun is meant in this case.
Naccaṃ nāma Dancing:
yaṃ kiñci naccaṃ. any kind of dancing.
Gītaṃ nāma Singing:
yaṃ kiñci gītaṃ. any kind of singing.
Vāditaṃ nāma Music:
yaṃ kiñci vāditaṃ. any kind of music.

Dassanāya gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. If she is on her way to see it, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Yattha ṭhitā passati vā suṇāti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Wherever she stands to see it or hear it, she commits an offense entailing confession. Dassanūpacāraṃ vijahitvā punappunaṃ passati vā suṇāti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Every time she moves outside the range of sight and then sees it or hears it again, she commits an offense entailing confession.

Ekamekaṃ dassanāya gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. If she is on her way to see one of the three, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Yattha ṭhitā passati vā suṇāti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Wherever she stands to see it or hear it, she commits an offense entailing confession. Dassanūpacāraṃ vijahitvā punappunaṃ passati vā suṇāti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Every time she moves outside the range of sight and then sees it or hears it again, she commits an offense entailing confession.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: ārāme ṭhitā passati vā suṇāti vā, if she sees it or hears it while standing in the monastery; bhikkhuniyā ṭhitokāsaṃ vā nisinnokāsaṃ vā nipannokāsaṃ vā āgantvā naccanti vā gāyanti vā vādenti vā, if the dancing, singing, or music comes to where the nun happens to stand, sit, or lie down; paṭipathaṃ gacchantī passati vā suṇāti vā, if she sees it or hears it while walking in the opposite direction; sati karaṇīye gantvā passati vā suṇāti vā, if she goes there because there is something to be done and she then sees it or hears it; āpadāsu, if there are dangers; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. The tenth training rule is finished.

Lasuṇavaggo paṭhamo. The first chapter on garlic is finished.
