Pācittiyakaṇḍa The chapter on offenses entailing confession

Kumāribhūtavagga The sub-chapter on unmarried girls

74. Ūnadvādasavassasikkhāpada 74. The training rule on less than twelve years

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo ūnadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpenti. At that time nuns who had less than twelve years of seniority gave the full ordination. Tā bālā honti abyattā na jānanti kappiyaṃ vā akappiyaṃ vā. They were ignorant and incompetent, and they did not know what was allowable and what was not. Saddhivihāriniyopi bālā honti abyattā; And their students, too, were ignorant and incompetent, na jānanti kappiyaṃ vā akappiyaṃ vā. and they did not know what was allowable and what was not.

Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The nuns of few desires complained and criticized them, “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo ūnadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpessantī”ti … pe … “How can nuns who have less than twelve years of seniority give the full ordination?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo ūnadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpentī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that nuns do this?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized them, “… kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo ūnadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpessanti. How can nuns who have less than twelve years of seniority give the full ordination? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Yā pana bhikkhunī ūnadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyan”ti. ‘If a nun who has less than twelve years of seniority gives the full ordination, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Yā panāti A:
yā yādisā … pe … whoever …
bhikkhunīti Nun:
… pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti. … The nun who has been given the full ordination by both complete Orders through a legal procedure consisting of one motion and three announcements that is irreversible and fit to stand—this sort of nun is meant in this case.
Ūnadvādasavassā nāma Who has less than twelve years of seniority:
appattadvādasavassā. who has not reached twelve years of seniority.
Vuṭṭhāpeyyāti Gives the full ordination:
upasampādeyya. gives the full ordination. “Vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti gaṇaṃ vā ācariniṃ vā pattaṃ vā cīvaraṃ vā pariyesati, sīmaṃ vā sammannati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. If, intending to give the full ordination, she searches for a group, a teacher, a bowl, or a robe, or she establishes a boundary, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Ñattiyā dukkaṭaṃ. After the motion, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Dvīhi kammavācāhi dukkaṭā. After each of the first two announcements, she commits an offense of wrong conduct. Kammavācāpariyosāne upajjhāyāya āpatti pācittiyassa. When the last announcement is finished, the preceptor commits an offense entailing confession; Gaṇassa ca ācariniyā ca āpatti dukkaṭassa. the group and the teacher commit an offense of wrong conduct.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: paripuṇṇadvādasavassā vuṭṭhāpeti, if she has twelve years of seniority and then gives the full ordination; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. The fourth training rule is finished.
