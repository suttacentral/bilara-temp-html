4. Jatumaṭṭhakasikkhāpada 4. The training rule on dildos

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarā purāṇarājorodhā bhikkhunīsu pabbajitā hoti. At that time a woman who had previously belonged to the king’s harem had been given the full ordination as a nun. Aññatarā bhikkhunī anabhiratiyā pīḷitā yena sā bhikkhunī tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā taṃ bhikkhuniṃ etadavoca— Another nun who was plagued by discontent went to that nun and said, “rājā kho, ayye, tumhe cirāciraṃ gacchati. “Venerable, the king only came to you at long intervals. Kathaṃ tumhe dhārethā”ti? How did you cope?”

“Jatumaṭṭhakena, ayye”ti. “I used a dildo, Venerable.”

“Kiṃ etaṃ, ayye, jatumaṭṭhakan”ti? “What’s a dildo?”

Atha kho sā bhikkhunī tassā bhikkhuniyā jatumaṭṭhakaṃ ācikkhi. And that nun described a dildo to her. Atha kho sā bhikkhunī jatumaṭṭhakaṃ ādiyitvā dhovituṃ vissaritvā ekamantaṃ chaḍḍesi. Then the other nun used a dildo, forgot to wash it, and disposed of it at a certain place. Bhikkhuniyo makkhikāhi samparikiṇṇaṃ passitvā evamāhaṃsu— The nuns saw that it was covered with flies, and they said, “kassidaṃ kamman”ti? “Who did this?”

Sā evamāha— “mayhidaṃ kamman”ti. “I did it,” she replied.

Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The nuns of few desires complained and criticized her, “kathañhi nāma bhikkhunī jatumaṭṭhakaṃ ādiyissatī”ti … pe … “How can a nun use a dildo?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī jatumaṭṭhakaṃ ādiyatī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that a nun did this?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized her, kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī jatumaṭṭhakaṃ ādiyissati. “How can a nun use a dildo? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Jatumaṭṭhake pācittiyan”ti. ‘If a nun uses a dildo, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Jatumaṭṭhakaṃ nāma A dildo:
jatumayaṃ kaṭṭhamayaṃ piṭṭhamayaṃ mattikāmayaṃ. made of resin, made of wood, made of flour, made of clay.
Ādiyeyyāti Uses:
samphassaṃ sādiyantī antamaso uppalapattampi muttakaraṇaṃ paveseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. if she consents to the contact and makes it enter her vagina, even if it is just a lotus leaf, she commits an offense entailing confession.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: ābādhapaccayā, if she does it because of sickness; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. The fourth training rule is finished.
