2.4.9.8 Sikkhamānaummaddāpanasikkhāpada 91-93. The training rules on getting trainee nuns/novice nuns/female lay people to massage

Origin story

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo sikkhamānāya ummaddāpentipi parimaddāpentipi. At that time the nuns were getting trainee nuns to massage and rub them … novice nuns to massage and rub them … female lay people to massage and rub them. Manussā vihāracārikaṃ āhiṇḍantā passitvā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— When people who were walking about the dwellings saw this, they complained and criticized them, “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo sikkhamānāya ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipi, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti. “How can nuns get female lay people to massage and rub them? They are just like lay people who indulge in sensual pleasures.”

Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. The nuns heard the complaints of those people. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— The nuns of few desires complained and criticized them, “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo sikkhamānāya ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipī”ti … pe … “How can nuns get female lay people to massage and rub them?” … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo sikkhamānāya ummaddāpentipi parimaddāpentipī”ti? “Is it true, monks, that nuns do this?”

“Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. “It’s true, Master.”

Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … The Buddha criticized them, “… kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo sikkhamānāya ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipi. How can nuns get female lay people to massage and rub them? Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … This will not give rise to confidence in those without it … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— And, monks, the nuns should recite this training rule thus:

Final ruling

“Yā pana bhikkhunī (sikkhamānāya…pe… sāmaṇeriyā…pe… ) gihiniyā ummaddāpeyya vā parimaddāpeyya vā, pācittiyan”ti. ‘If a nun gets (a trainee nun … a novice nun …) a laywoman to massage her or to rub her, she commits an offense entailing confession.’”

Definitions

Yā panāti A:
yā yādisā … pe … whoever …
bhikkhunīti Nun:
… pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti. … The nun who has been given the full ordination by both complete Orders through a legal procedure consisting of one motion and three announcements that is irreversible and fit to stand—this sort of nun is meant in this case.
Sikkhamānā nāma A trainee nun:
dve vassāni chasu dhammesu sikkhitasikkhā. one who has trained for two years in the six rules.
Sāmaṇerī nāma A novice nun:
dasasikkhāpadikā. a female training in the ten training rules.
Gihinī nāma A laywoman:
agārinī vuccati. a female who lives in a house is what is meant.
Ummaddāpeyya vāti Gets […] to massage her:
ummaddāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. if she gets massaged, she commits an offense entailing confession.
Parimaddāpeyya vāti Gets […] to rub her:
sambāhāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. if she gets rubbed, she commits an offense entailing confession.

Non-offenses

Anāpatti— There is no offense: gilānāya, if she is sick; āpadāsu, if there are dangers; ummattikāya, if she is insane; ādikammikāyāti. if she is the first offender.

Aṭṭhama-navama-dasamasikkhāpadāni niṭṭhitāni. The eighth, ninth, and tenth training rules are finished.
